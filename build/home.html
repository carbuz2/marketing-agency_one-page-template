Web Applications for portfolio management

Do it faster, do it better!

Our asset management software will support you in your daily basis tasks such as asset and portfolio management, automatic flows with custodian banks and brokers, back and middle office activities, risk management, and report generation.

We developed a web application that your team can run in any device (from pc to tablet and smartphone).

Your team will use the same application (with the possibility to set different permissions for each person of team) using a unique database, saving time and money.

Software Modules

Let us help you to better organize your work!

  • Transaction

    Save all transactions into the system through an excel with pre-established fields or using custom files (even pdf!) provided directly from your broker or your bank. If you need, you can manually enter the details of a transaction. The software allows the calculation of commissions of each transaction as well as taxes and accrued interests. The inserted transactions can then be filtered by date / entry field in order to facilitate their management.

  • Orders

    As for transactions, we have a register where you can save all your orders. Orders can be imported manually, by an excel uploaded by the user (even attached to a predefined mail). This module provides for the possibility of sending orders - by mail or fax - to brokers. In addition, there is a function to reconcile orders with the confirmations (in pdf, text or excel format) received from the broker or bank.

  • Reports

    Create your pdf custom report, with your logo selecting all the tables and the charts you want to plot! Or - if you prefer an excel - you can select and rearrange all the columns you want to export for any provided table. Create a good looking report with the needed information has never been so easy!

  • Positions

    The Software calculates the portfolio positions resulting from all the transactions entered by the managers. Additionally, we provide a specific report highlighting any differences between the official bank position at a certain date, on the one hand, and the position registered in the software, on the other. Through this module it is possible to check ex-post that the selected limits are respected (crucial task for UCITS funds for example).

  • Risk Management

    Check all that your current positions (ex post control) or your orders (ex-ante control) respect the limits you selected.

  • And much more...

    The platform provide many other functionalities: you can schedule tasks (like for example sending custom reports to certain mail at a selected hours), restore records that you delete by mistake, present interactive charts to your clients, generate invoices, find selected assets in all the portfolios, associate file to a particular entity (clients, brokers, trades, orders, etc)... and much more!

We provide an effective affordable software solution for managing private portfolios or funds. Our product is ideal to support asset managers and advisors in all their most important activities. It works not only for desktop but also for tablets and smartphones. Additionally, the product can be easely customized according to your needs!

How It Works

A recap of the main functionalities

  • Step 1 — Insert your orders

    Insert your orders throw a file template: the software will check if all the limits you set up are respected giving you the possibility to send them to your broker.

  • Step 2 — Insert and reconcile transactions

    Once you receive the confirmation of the executed order, the software will import it and will automatically reconcile it with the pending orders. Our product allows you to generate custom emails and files for the most common middle and back office operations.

  • Step 3 — Be focus on your portfolios

    Portfolios will be automatically updated with the last available data, providing you useful information about your portfolios (like returns, post compliance reports, expected tax/trading fees, expected management/performance fees, etc)

  • Step 4 — Enjoy our reports

    Custom excel/pdf reports can be extracted from the platform according to the client needs.
