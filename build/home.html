Web Applications for portfolio management

Our asset management software will help you seamlessly and effortlessly running daily basis tasks such as asset and portfolio management, as it automates workflows of custodian banks and brokers, back and middle office activities, risk management, as well as report generation.

Our web application is perfectly optimised for cross platform usage so that your team can run it on any device (computers, tablets and smartphones).

You can set up permission levels for each team member or department for access and they can still use the samedatabase, saving time and money.

Software Modules

Let us help you to better organize your work!

  • Transactions

    Save all transactions in the system through an excel with pre-established fields or using custom files (even pdf!) provided directly from your broker or your bank. If you need, you can manually enter details of a transaction. The software allows calculating commissions of each transaction as well as taxes and accrued interests. The inserted transactions can then be filtered by date / entry field for easy management.

  • Orders

    As for transactions, we have a registry where you can save all your orders. Orders can be imported manually, by uploading excel files by the the user (even attached to a predefined mail). This module makes it possible to send orders - by mail or fax - to brokers. In addition, there is a function to reconcile orders with the confirmations (in pdf, text or excel format) received from the broker or bank.

  • Reports

    Create custom pdf reports, with your logo including all tables and charts you want to plot! Or - if you prefer an excel file - you can select and rearrange all the columns you want to export for any given table. Creating a professional looking report with essential information has never been so easy!

  • Positions

    The Software calculates aggregate portfolio positions resulting from individual or batch transactions as entered by managers. Additionally, it generates a specific report highlighting any differences between the official broker or bank position at a certain date on one hand, and the position registered in the software, on the other. Through this module it is possible to check ex-post whether selected limits are respected (a crucial task for UCITS funds for example).

  • Risk Management

    Check all of your current positions (ex post control) and your orders (ex-ante control) ensuring that the limits you selected are respected.

  • And much more...

    The platform has many more useful functions: you can schedule tasks (such as sending custom reports to certain email addresses at a selected time), restore records that were deleted by mistake, present interactive charts to your clients, generate invoices, find selected assets in all portfolios, associate files to particular entities (clients, brokers, trades, orders, etc)... and much more!

How It Works

A recap of the main functionalities

  • Step 1 — Insert your orders

    Insert your orders throw a file template: the software will check if all the limits you set up are respected giving you the possibility to send them to your broker.

  • Step 2 — Insert and reconcile transactions

    Once you receive the confirmation of the executed order, the software will import it and will automatically reconcile it with the pending orders. Our product allows you to generate custom emails and files for the most common middle and back office operations.

  • Step 3 — Be focus on your portfolios

    Portfolios will be automatically updated with the last available data, providing you useful information about your portfolios (like returns, post compliance reports, expected tax/trading fees, expected management/performance fees, etc)

  • Step 4 — Enjoy our reports

    Custom excel/pdf reports can be extracted from the platform according to your needs.
